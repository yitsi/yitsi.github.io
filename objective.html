i. Detect low concentration of second-hand smoke by sensing three toxic chemicals: CO, NO and formaldehyde: (a) detect NO by using NsrR-regulate promoter PyeaR; (b) detect CO by CooA-sensitive promoter PCooF; (c) detect formaldehyde by frmR-control promoter PfrmR.

ii. Using siRNA to implement a three-input OR gate, as long as a toxic is detected, siRNA will bind to its correlated target sequence and do self-cleave, stop producing LacI.

iii. Use amplifier to strengthen the production of reporter gene GFP to increase precision.
